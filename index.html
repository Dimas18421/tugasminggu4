About Me

Hello, my name is Dimas Prioseto. I am a student currently studying Information Systems at Universitas Muhammadiyah Sumatera Utara (UMSU). I have a strong interest in technology, especially in programming and website development. I am interested in learning how digital systems work and how technology can be used to help solve everyday problems. At the moment, I am learning and improving my skills in programming, especially using Python, and also learning to create simple websites with HTML. During this learning process, I have tried to create several simple projects to understand how applications and websites are built from the beginning. Through these activities, I can practice logical thinking, problem solving, and writing more organized code. As an Information Systems student , I am also interested in understanding how technology, data, and business processes can work together to create more effective and efficient digital solutions. I believe that technology plays an important role in helping people work, communicate, and access information. I always try to continue learning and developing my skills in the field of technology. In the future, I hope to become a developer who can create useful applications and contribute positively to society. Outside of coding, I also enjoy learning new technologies and gaining new experiences so that my skills can continue to grow.
